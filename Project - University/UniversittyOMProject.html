1)Class Name:
University:
Attributes:
universityName,address, email, contact

Subclass
Course
Attributes:
type(bachelors/masters..) ,name, length and fee.

Methods:
getfees (check if fees is expensive)

2)Class Name
Teacher:
Attributes:
firstName, surName, id,experience and salary
Methods:
salaryRange(if salary is good based on experience)
training (If the experiene is below 2 years, then the teacher has to undergo a training)

3)Class
Student:
Attributes:
firstName, surName, id, schoolScores and bankDetails
Methods:
previousScores (checks if the scores got in high school is good or average)
